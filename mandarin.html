台湾研学旅行日常活动

Daily Activities during EduTrip in Taiwan

三月八日, 星期六的活动

Activities on 8 March, Saturday

Tao Garden 酒店

Tao Garden Hotel

三月八日, 飞抵桃园国际机场，之后我们就到桃花园酒店住了一晚。
On 8 March, I landed at Taoyuan International Airport and headed to Tao Garden Hotel where we stayed at for 1 night.

三月九日, 星期日的活动

Activities on 9 March, Sunday

圣家天主教堂

Holy Family Catholic Church

三月九日, 我们到圣家堂参加晨间弥撒，之后又参观了自行车文化博物馆，了解了很多关于自行车的历史，傍晚时分，我们便飞往高雄，接下来的三天，我们将在此停留。
On 9 March, we went to Holy Family Catholic Church to attend a morning mass. Then, we visited the Cycling Culture Museum, where we learned a lot about this history of bicycles. In the evening, we transfered to Kaohshiung where we will stay at for the next three days.

三月十日, 星期一的活动

Activities on 10 March, Monday

文藻大学

Wenzao University

这天，我们去了文藻大学。在那里，我们见到了大学的学生，并参观了学校的校园。
On this day, we went to Wenzao University. There, we meet the university's students and explore the school's campus.

Tzu Chi Foundation Resource Center

我们还去了慈济基金会中心，在那里我们做社区服务，帮助他们分类不同类型的垃圾，以及我们可以做些什么来保护环境。
We also went to Tzu Chi Foundation center where we did community serive by helping them separate different type of trashes as well as in what ways can we do to protect the environment.

我做的凤梨酥

The pineapple cake I made

晚上，我们去了凤梨酥工厂。在那里，他们教我们如何制作自己的凤梨酥！
In the evening, we went to Pineapple Cake Factory. There, they taught us how to make our own pineapple cakes!

三月十一日, 星期二的活动

Activities on 11 March, Tuesday

道明学校

St. Dominic School

这天，我们去了道明学校。在那里，我们做社区服务，陪伴老人，与他们交谈，与他们一起玩游戏以及做其他活动。
On this day, we went to St. Dominic School. There, we did community service where we accompanied old people by talking to them and playing games with them as well as doing other activities.

国家科学技术博物馆

National Science and Technology Museum

中午，我们去了国立科学技术博物馆，在那里我们看到了很多展览，主要是关于台湾的科技。
In the afternoon, we went to National Science and Technology Museum where we saw a lot of exhibitions, mainly about the technology in Taiwan.

从山上俯瞰旗津岛

View from the hill of Cijin Island

下午，我们乘渡轮去了旗津岛。在那里，我们爬上了一座小山，欣赏了非常美丽的景色。
In the evening, we went to Cijin Island by riding a ferry. There, we climbed a small hill to look at a really beautiful view.

Pier 2 Art Center的艺术装置之一

One of the art installations that can be found in Pier 2 Art Center

晚上，我们参观了Pier 2 Art Center，在那里我们看到了很多有趣的艺术装置。
At night, we visited Pier 2 Art Center where we saw a lot of interesting art installations.

三月十二日, 星期三的活动

Activities on 12 March, Wednesday

这天我们又去了道明学校。在那里，我们和学校的学生一起参观了学校。我们看到了学校提供的所有设施，了解了在那里当学生的感受。
On this day we went to St. Dominic School again. There we toured around the school with the school's students. We saw all the facilities provided and learn what it is like to be a student there.

西门町夜市

Ximending Night Market

傍晚飞往台北，游览西门町夜市
In the evening, we transferred to Taipei and visited Ximending night market.

在那里可以找到的纪念品

Some souvenirs that can be found there

这里有很多食物可供品尝，也有很多纪念品可供选择。
There were a lot of foods to try as well as a lot of souvenirs to choose from.

三月十三日, 星期四的活动

Activities on 13 March, Thursday

国立故宫博物院

National Palace Museum

这天我们去了故宫博物院，看到了过去台湾人使用过的物品。
On this day, we went to the National Palace Museum where we see objects used by Taiwanese people in the past.

Chiang Kai Shek的纪念堂

Chiang Kai Shek Memorial Hall

我们还去了Chiang Kai Shek的纪念堂。
We also went to Chiang Kai Shek Memorial Hall.

Chiang Kai Shek的雕像

Statue of Chiang Kai Shek

在那里，我们可以看到他的一座很大的雕像。
There, we can see a really big statue of him.

台北 101

Taipei 101

傍晚时分，我们去了台北101。
Around evening, we went to Taipei 101.

从台北 101 观景台看到的景色

View from the observatory deck at Taipei 101

我们乘坐非常快的电梯到达大楼顶部，从那里我们可以从上面欣赏到台北的美景。
There we rode a really fast elevator to the top of the building where we get to see a really good view of Taipei from above.

三月十四日, 星期五的活动

Activities on 14 March, Friday

野柳地质公园

Yehliu Geopark

这天，我们去了野柳地质公园，在那里看到了很多不同自然形状的岩石。
On this day, we went to Yehliu Geopark where we saw a lot of different naturally shaped rocks.

爬九份老街的楼梯

Climbing the stairs at Jiufen Old Street

下午我们去了九份老街。那里有很多楼梯要爬。在那里，你可以买到纪念品、小吃和饮料。
In the afternoon we went to Jiufen Old Street. There were a lot of stairs to climb there. There, you can buy souvenir, snacks, and drink shops here and there.

我和朋友们在十分老街放灯

Me and my friends releasing our lantern at Shifen Old Street

傍晚时分，我们到十分老街放飞写有我们愿望的灯笼。
Later in the evening, we went to Shifen Old Street to release lanterns that are written with our wishes.

三月十五日, 星期六的活动

Activity on 15 March, Saturday

今天我们从台北飞抵雅加达。
Today, we flew from Taipei and arrived at Jakarta.

描述我来自台湾的朋友

Desbribing My Friend from Taiwan

这是我的新朋友, 她的名字叫Sherry。她长得很漂亮。她瓜子脸, 小小的的眼晴、高高的鼻子和小小的嘴巴。她瘦瘦的。她头发是黑色的, 又长又直。
This is my new friend, her name is Sherry. She is very beautiful. She has an oval face, small eyes, a high nose and a small mouth. She is thin. Her hair is black, long and straight.
